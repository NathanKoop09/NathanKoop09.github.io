About
 Hello, I'm Nathan, An honors graduate student from the Grant Macewan Computer Science program with a strong background in Mathematics. From a young age I had a strong curiosity for computers and how they worked. This curiosity drives my passion for the world of Software Development and Game Design as well as fueling my desire to continue to learn as much as I can about the vast realm of technology.
Skills
 Over the course of my academic life I have honed my skills in both high level languages; such as C, C++, C#, and Python, as well as low level languages such as winx64 ASM. I have also become proficient with various programming patterns and paradigms including Object Oriented, Procedural, and Data Oriented programming.
Analytical
 Mathematics and Computer Science go hand-in-hand: both require solving a problem by using a set of tools in a unique way to come up with a solution. My background in Mathematics gives me a competitive edge in problem solving skills and analytical reasoning allowing me to find clever solutions to complex problems and prove their correctness, as well as find optimizations in existing algorithms to improve performance.

Projects

Animal Crisis

A strategic board game using peer-to-peer matchmaking so you can play with your friends.

Eternal Drift

A fast paced racing game with custom physics and collision system to ensure accurate collisions at arbitrarily high speeds.

Slope V

A story driven role playing game with three large dynamic and scalable systems for the battles, cutscenes, and inventory.

Space Raiders

A game built from scratch in 48 hours. Includes custom sprites as well as custom box and sphere colliders.

Slopehou

A fast paced and difficult endless runner that uses spawn pooling and object ordering in order to perform needed physics calculation without lag regardless of speed.

Love Fun and Robots

A two player platformer with ten levels that was built as part of a two day game jam. Progress is saved using a Binary Formatter.

Tower of Ascension

A 3D platformer where you try to climb a tower using obstacles such as: wall jumps, ledge grabbing, and rotating platforms.

Super Tank World

A tank game where you fight your friends in a randomly generated maze, generated to avoid wide open spaces and have more loops.

C.U.B.E.R.T

A 3D platformer built from scratch in OpenGL. Uses the Blinn-Phong lighting model to light textures and Z-buffers to correctly display transparency.

Termino

A bullet hell game that uses the terminal as a display. It uses Multithreading to ensure a smooth visual experience.
