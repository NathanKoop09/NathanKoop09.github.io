Tower of Ascension

Technical Details

The game uses several types of physics based obstacles to provide a fresh and engaging climb up the tower. These obstacles include: wall jump sections, ledge jumps, pipe climbs, rail gliding, cannons, rotating platforms, flipping platforms, spike and tar traps, as well as conveyer belts.
The boss fight at the end of the game uses several of these obstacles in a dynamic fashion to build an engaging final encounter to test the skills of the player.

Project Description

A slow paced 3d platformer where the player awakens in an unfamiliar tower without their memory. Without anything better to do they decide that they must climb to the top of this strange tower.
The atmosphere of the tower desolate with an emphasis placed on internal contemplation. Perhaps if you reach the towers top you will know the truth about yourself and this tower. Make sure to remain focused as the tower is very punishing to those who lose focus and fall to the floors below.
Climb two floors and face the boss at the top to complete your journey up the tower.
