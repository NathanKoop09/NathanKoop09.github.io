Super Tank World

Technical Details

All game modes, except the Sumo wrestling mode, take place in a randomly generated maze. This maze is generated using a custom maze generating algorithm that builds off of a randomized depth first search algorithm, in order to build loops and limit the amount of wide open spaces.

Project Description

A fast paced, arcade style, tank game where you can play locally with up to three players. Play through four different game modes: Classic tank battles, King of the Hill, Orb Collection, and Sumo wrestling. The game keeps track of your overall score so you can see who among your friends is the best at this sharp-shooting tank game.
