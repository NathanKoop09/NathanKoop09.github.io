Space Raiders

Technical Details

This game was part of a challenge to create as good a game as possible in a single weekend. Everything was built from scratch in approximately 20 hours of active development.
The bullets feature a custom collision detection method to ensure that the players hitbox is only a one pixel radius. The two types of colliders implemented are a circular collider and a box collider that works at arbitrary angles.

Project Description

A bullet hell game in which you need to take down wave after wave of enemy ships. Don't take too long to destroy them or else they'll get a chance to use their power move, making it much harder to survive.
Each ship you shoot down earns you one point. The further you go into space, the more types of ships you'll encounter. There are nine different ships to encounter, can you get to them all?
