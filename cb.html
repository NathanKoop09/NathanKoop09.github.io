C.U.B.E.R.T

Technical Details

This game was made from scratch using OpenGl. All models are stored as elements in a json list representing the level data. The lighting system implemented is the blinn-phong model and interacts with the textures of a given object. Z-buffers are used to correctly implement transparency. The collision used is a custom box-to-box collider method.

Project Description

A short 3D platformer where you play as a green cube known as Cubert who is on a mission to reach the moon. Escape your spaceship and navigate the perils of outer space to reach the moon.
Use WASD to move and IJKL to look around. Press Z to toggle between third person and first person view.
